This is what I do.

  • Web Design

    I aim to create experiences that look and function beautifully across anything that can access the web. I am a web designer based in beautiful Pittsburgh, PA.

  • Writing

    I write about responsive design, atomic design, and more on my blog. I'm writing a book called Atomic Design, and have contributed pieces to a number of publications, magazines and books.

  • Speaking

    I'm extremely passionate about what I do and love talking about where the Web is going. I speak about design, development and more at conferences all around the world.

  • Workshops

    I give workshops about responsive web design, atomic design, the modern Web design process, and more at companies and conferences.

  • Consulting

    I help organizations tackle the ever-shifting multi-device Web landscape, and can help with strategy, process, design, and development.

  • This Is Responsive

    This Is Responsive is a site that collects resources, news and emerging patterns to help people create great responsive web experiences.

  • Styleguides.io

    Co-created with Anna Debenham, Styleguides.io is a website that shares examples, tools, and resources about style guides and pattern libraries. We also co-host a podcast!

  • Pattern Lab

    Dave Olsen and I created Pattern Lab, a tool to help people create and maintain effective interface design systems.

  • Death to Bullshit

    Death to Bullshit is a rallying cry to rid the world of bullshit and demand experiences that respect people and their time.

  • WTF Mobile Web

    Co-curated with Jen Simmons, WTF Mobile Web collects examples of what not to do when creating web experiences in this mobile age.

  • WTF QR Codes

    QR codes can be hilarious. Craig Villamor and I collect the most ridiculous implementations of QR codes out in the wild.

  • Music

    I'm primarily a bass player, though I also enjoy playing drums, guitar and keyboards (tabletops when nothing else is available). I play and record with my wonderful wife.

  • Art

    Art has been a passion my whole life and I will always pursue it in some way shape or form. I enjoy painting, drawing, sculpting and designing.
